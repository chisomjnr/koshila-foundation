What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lives with you

Healthy Food

We supply numerous children with Healthy food and snacks to help promote hope and joy amongst the children.

Pure Water

We help reduce the negative impact of the unclean water used by these children daily by providing them with proper drinkable water.

Health Care

We provide these children with proper health care, reducing the rates of illness (like malaria & typhoid) and untimely deaths. With your help we can help curb more illnesses.

Primary Education

We provide these children with important basic knowledge and education on subjects like English, Maths & Basic Science.

Residence Facilities

With your help we can provide these children with important basic anemities like usable toilets, water plumbing systems, solar power & so much more.

Social Care

We create fun events and seminars for thes children to help boost self esteem, raise awareness and distribute hope amongst the children.
